Tribute to Ophra Winfrey

Ophra Winfrey

An extra ordinary women!

Born of the 29 January 1954 in a small town of Kosciusko, Mississippi, raised by a single mother Vernita Lee.

Ophra's Time line

  • 1954 January 29 She was born in Kosciusko, Mississippi
  • 1954-1960 she was living with her grandmother in the rural arears in poverty
  • 1960-1968 Moved from Milwaukee to leave with her mother,half-sister and half-brother
  • 1968 she ran away from due to abuse by her uncle and she become wild, during this time she gave birth to a prematured child that passed away soon after birth.
  • 1970 she won a scholarship to Tennessee State University
  • 1971 she won Miss Black Tennesse beauty pageant
  • 1973 She graduated from Tennessee State University with a Degree in Speech and Performing Arts and became the first black TV news anchor reporter with WTVF-TV.
  • 1992 She Won the best Talk Show Host at the Daytime Emmy Awards
  • 2003 She was the narrator in the documentary Unchained Memories: Readings from the Slave Narratives.
  • 2006 February 9: Signed 3-year contract with XM Satellite Radio to establish a new radio channel based in Chicago
  • 2011-till present hosts super-Soul-Sunday
